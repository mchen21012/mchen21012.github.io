WHO AM I?

VIDEO GAME DEVELOPER    |    HUMANITIES EDUCATOR    |    CREATIVE WRITER

I am a Master of Engineering (M.Eng) student in Computer Science at Cornell University's College of Engineering. My concentration is in Natural Language Processing. For my undergraduate studies, I majored in English and Information Science with minors in Computer Science and Education at Cornell University's College of Arts and sciences. As someone who has a strong interest in game development, I hope to write video game storylines and design games in the future. Furthermore, I am passionate about web development and hope to create websites that are accessible and meet the needs of as many people as possible. I am also a lover of classic literature and history; thus, I hope to spread my passion for the humanities to the future generation of students.
