WHO AM I?

VIDEO GAME DEVELOPER    |    HUMANITIES EDUCATOR    |    CREATIVE WRITER

I am a senior at Cornell University majoring in English and Information Science and minoring in Computer Science and Education. Concurrently, I am pursuing a Master of Engineering (M.Eng) in Computer Science with a focus on artificial intelligence and video game development. As someone who has a strong interest in game creation, I hope to write video game storylines and design games in the future. I am also a lover of classic literature and history; thus, I hope to spread my passion for the humanities to the future generation of students.
